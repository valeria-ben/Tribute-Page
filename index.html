Diana, Princess of Wales

Queen of people's hearts

"I don't go by the rule book... I lead from the heart, not the head."

  • 1961

    Diana is born July 1 at Park House, Sandringham.

  • 1969

    Diana is 7-years-old when her parents get divorced. She learns to relate to anyone who is upset in their famlily life. She said: "whether it be stepfather syndrome or mother or whatever, I understand it. Been there, done it."

  • 1970

    Diana enrolls at Riddlesworth Hall: an all-girls boarding school in Norfolk.

  • 1973

    Diana joins the West Heath Girls' School in Kent. She stood out in music and sports, but not on academics.

  • 1975

    Diana's grandfather died and her father inherit the title Earl of Spencer. Consecuently, she is given the title of Lady, which turns into nickname Lady Di.

  • 1977

    Diana and Prince Charles meet for the first time.

  • 1981

    Diana and Prince Charles announce their engagement on February 24.

  • 1981

    On July 29, Diana and Charles get married at St. Paul's Cathedral. Her title is now Her Royal Highness The Princess of Wales.

  • 1982

    Diana and Charle's first child, Prince William Arthur Philip Louis, is born at London's St. Mary's Hospital.

  • 1984

    Charles and Diana's second son, Prince Henry Charles Albert David, is born.

  • 1989

    Diana confronts Camilla Parker Bowles on a birthday party about her affair with Charles. She said: "I would just like you to know that I know exactly what is going on".

  • 1992

    Diana's father dies of a heart attack in London.

  • 1992

    On December, the Prince and Princess of Wales officialy separate.

  • 1993

    Tapes of audio recordings of phone conversations between Charles and Camilla surfaced, confirming the rumours.

  • 1997

    Diana dies after a car accident, in which the intoxicated driver was driving three times over the legal limit. Her funeral is watched by 2.5 billion people around the world.
